1

Mean: The Average

The mean is what we commonly call the "average." It acts as the balancing point of your data, like the fulcrum of a seesaw.

Definition & Formula

The mean (x̄) is calculated by summing all values and dividing by the total count.

Mean = (Sum of all values) / (Number of values)

Example: Student Data Usage

Data (GB): 2, 3, 5, 7, 8

  • Step 1: Sum = 2 + 3 + 5 + 7 + 8 = 25
  • Step 2: Divide by 5 = 5 GB

Mean: -

Limitation: Sensitivity to Outliers

The mean is easily pulled by extreme values (outliers).

Example: If one student used 100 GB instead of 8 GB, the mean jumps from 5 GB to 23.4 GB! This no longer represents the "typical" student.

2

Median: The Middle Ground

The median is the middle value when the data is arranged in order. It splits the data into two equal halves.

Odd Number of Values

The median is the exact middle number.

2, 3, 5, 7, 8

Median = 5

Even Number of Values

The median is the average of the two middle numbers.

2, 3, 5, 7, 8, 9

Median = (5 + 7) / 2 = 6

Why use Median? Robustness!

The median is NOT swayed by outliers. In our outlier example (2, 3, 5, 7, 100), the median is still 5.

3

Mode: The Most Frequent

The mode is simply the value that appears most often. It's the "popular vote" of statistics.

Unimodal

One clear winner.

12, 15, 15, 15, 18

Mode: 15

Bimodal

Two values tie.

12, 12, 14, 15, 15

Modes: 12 & 15

No Mode

No repeats.

2, 5, 8, 9, 11

None

Mean vs. Median: The Ultimate Showdown

Scenario: The CEO's Salary

RoleSalary (Monthly)
Intern₹20,000
Junior Dev₹30,000
Senior Dev₹40,000
Manager₹40,000
Designer₹35,000
CEO (Outlier)₹5,00,000

Mean: ₹1,10,833

Suggests "typical" pay is >1 Lakh. Misleading.

Median: ₹37,500

Reflects normal employee pay. Accurate.

Quick Decision Guide

MeasureBest Used When...Sensitive to Outliers?
MeanSymmetrical data, comparing groupsYes
MedianSkewed data (income, prices)No
ModeCategorical data (most popular)No

Real-World Applications

Spam Detection

If an email has 100 links while the median is 2, it's flagged as spam.

Netflix Recommendations

Uses median watch time to recommend movies vs sitcoms.

Indian Context Examples

Electricity Bills

Use Median: Avoid skew from heavy AC users to find "normal" bill.

IT Salaries

Use Median: Founders earn exponentially more; median shows typical engineer pay.
